Purpose

Every student should be able to easily access and know about the many activities they can get involved in at their school. Thus, the intention for the 'CBHS Extracurricular Hub' is to part of an effort to extend equal educational opportunities among all students, no matter their background.

About the Developer

Hi, I'm Taylor and I'm and senior at Clear Brook! This website was developed as part of my Independent Study Mentorship where I researched the digital gap between socioeconomic classes and how that gap applies to the school setting within extracurriculars. Equal education and computer science are two subjects I am truly passionate about, thus I hoped to merge the two to help make an impact.
